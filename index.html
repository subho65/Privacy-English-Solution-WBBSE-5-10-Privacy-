Privacy Policy for WBBSE 5-10 English App

Effective Date: June 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Subhojati Dutta ("Developer", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the WBBSE 5-10 English App (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the requirements of the Indus Appstore and applicable Indian data protection laws.

1. Information We Collect

This App is designed to be a lightweight, offline-first educational tool. We do not track your browsing activity, location, or collect identifiable device metrics (like IMEI or MAC addresses).

  • Voluntary Data: The only personal information we collect is what you explicitly provide when using the "Send Feedback" feature. This includes your Name, Email Address, and your Message.
  • App Permissions: The App requires INTERNET permission solely to load external study resources (HTML iframes) and to submit your feedback to our secure database.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected via the feedback form is used strictly to:

  • Read and respond to your suggestions or bug reports.
  • Improve the App's study materials and user experience.

3. Local Data Storage

Your App preferences, such as text reading scale adjustments and UI settings, are processed and stored locally on your device using LocalStorage. We do not sync or store these personal settings on external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

To provide core functionalities, the App utilizes secure third-party services:

  • Supabase: Used exclusively as a secure backend database to receive and store user feedback.
  • Web Iframes: External textbook solutions or reference sheets may be served as direct web interfaces via standard HTML iframes.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertising networks or third-party marketers.

5. Children's Privacy

The App provides educational content based on the West Bengal Board syllabus for Classes 5 to 10. We do not require users to create an account, log in, or mandate the submission of personal data to use the core features. The feedback tool is optional and is intended to be used by students with the guidance of parents, guardians, or teachers. We do not knowingly profile minors.

6. Data Retention and Deletion Rights

We retain feedback data only for as long as necessary to address your queries or improve the App. You have the right to request the deletion of your data at any time.

To request the removal of your Name, Email, and Message from our database, please contact the developer via the in-app feedback form, or email the developer directly, stating "Data Deletion Request". We will process your request promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.
